GPU Related Requirements 
========================
* Tensorflow 1.1 with GPU support (https://www.tensorflow.org/install/install_linux#nvidia_requirements_to_run_tensorflow_with_gpu_support)
* CUDA Version 8.0.61
* Trained on Nvidia Tesla K80 (12 GB RAM), Driver version: 387.12

It may take a few hours to complete the training. You can see the estimated completion time in the console once the training begins.
